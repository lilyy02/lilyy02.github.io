INTERACTIVE MEDIA ASSIGNMENT 1:
INTERACTIVE RESEARCH PROJECT

This is a review of the interactive web project "The Man Whose Work We Don't Notice" - available at https://brooomstick.com/

Upon visiting the site, my attention was immediately caught by an animated loading screen. Bold green capital lettering in a sans serif font was centred in my view, with elipses gradually appearing which functioned as a progress bar. Images of different figures also moved across the screen, maintaining my attention as the webpage loaded.
